Data sorted by total filing volume. Use the column headers as a mental scroll guide; the table is plain HTML so browser find (Ctrl-F) works for jumping to a specific district.

Pre-DOJ note: Pre-guidance counts cover up to 35 years of historical filings (earliest in dataset: 1991). Post-guidance counts cover roughly 17 months (Nov 2022 – Apr 2026). The "Post share" column shows what fraction of each district's total was filed after Nov 17, 2022 — districts where post-share is near 100% reflect both the recent surge and historical RECAP coverage gaps.

Districts not in the dataset

Of the 94 U.S. bankruptcy districts, 7 are not currently represented in the dataset. Possible reasons: limited RECAP archive coverage for those courts, very low historical § 523(a)(8) AP volume, or differences in case-name conventions that bypass the defendant-pattern search. The dataset will grow as RECAP coverage expands.
